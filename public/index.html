How to Mine on Pastella Pool

1. Download a Compatible Miner

You can use the official Pastella miner or any miner that supports the Velora algorithm:

  • Pastella Miner: Official GPU miner optimized for Velora
  • XMRig: Multi-algorithm CPU/GPU miner with Velora support
  • Other miners: Any miner supporting Velora algorithm

2. Configure Your Miner

Use these connection settings in your miner configuration:

Pool URL: stratum+tcp://localhost:3333
Username: your-wallet-address.worker-name
Password: x
Algorithm: velora

3. Start Mining

Run your miner with the configured settings. The pool will automatically:

  • Assign work based on your miner's capabilities
  • Validate your submitted shares
  • Credit your account for valid work
  • Process payouts when you reach the minimum threshold

4. Monitor Your Progress

Use this dashboard to track:

  • Your current hashrate and share submission rate
  • Total shares submitted and accepted
  • Estimated earnings and pending payouts
  • Pool statistics and network difficulty

5. Receive Payouts

Payouts are processed automatically when you reach the minimum threshold of 0.001 PSTL. The pool fee is 1%.
